Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

Your files

All image and PDF processing on Orisod happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. The files you upload, convert, compress, crop, or resize are never sent to our servers and are never seen, stored, or accessed by us in any way. When you close the tab, the file is gone from memory — there is nothing to delete on our end because it was never there.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand overall site usage — for example, how many people visit, which tools are used most, and which pages are viewed. This is limited to anonymous, aggregated usage data such as:

  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • General location (country/city level, not precise)
  • Device and browser type
  • How you arrived at the site (search engine, direct link, etc.)

Google Analytics may use cookies to collect this information. This data is never linked to the contents of any file you process on Orisod.

Advertising

Orisod does not currently show ads. If we introduce advertising in the future (for example, through Google AdSense), this policy will be updated to describe what data any ad provider collects, and third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

Cookies

Our own tools do not set any cookies — everything runs client-side without needing to remember anything about you between visits. Google Analytics may set cookies as described above. You can block or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings.

Third-party services

Orisod's tools use open-source JavaScript libraries (such as PDF.js, jsPDF, and heic2any) that run entirely within your browser to perform conversions. These libraries do not transmit your files anywhere.

Children's privacy

Orisod is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
