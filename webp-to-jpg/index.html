What is a WEBP file?

WEBP is an image format developed by Google that provides smaller file sizes while maintaining high image quality. Many websites use WEBP to make pages load faster, but not all applications, older devices, or software support it — that's why converting to JPG is often necessary.

When should you use this tool?

  • You downloaded a WEBP image and your photo editor won't open it
  • An app or platform doesn't accept WEBP uploads, only JPG or PNG
  • You want to share the image with someone whose device doesn't support WEBP
  • You need to print the image and your print software requires JPG

Frequently asked questions

Why can't I open WEBP files in some programs?
WEBP is a newer format, and some older or less common software hasn't added support for it yet. Converting to JPG solves this instantly.
Will converting to JPG reduce quality?
There may be a small quality difference since JPG and WEBP use different compression methods, but for most images the difference is not noticeable.
Are my files uploaded anywhere?
No, the conversion happens locally in your browser using JavaScript.
Can I convert multiple WEBP files at once?
Yes, select or drag multiple files and download them individually or all together.
