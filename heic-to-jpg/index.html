What is a HEIC file?

HEIC is the image format used by default on iPhones and iPads. It keeps high image quality while using less storage space than JPG. Many computers, websites, and apps outside the Apple ecosystem cannot open HEIC files directly, which is why converting to JPG is often necessary.
