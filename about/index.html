About Orisod

Free, simple, privacy-friendly tools for images and PDFs.

What we do

Orisod Labs builds free online tools that help people convert, compress, resize, crop, and optimize their images and PDFs — without accounts, installs, or unnecessary complexity. Every tool is designed to solve one specific task well, quickly, and for free.

Why we built this

We were tired of "free" online converters that bury the actual tool behind ads, require sign-up just to download a file, or quietly upload your files to a server without saying so. We wanted something simpler: open the page, use the tool, get your file — that's it.

Our approach to privacy

Every tool on Orisod runs entirely inside your browser. When you convert or edit a file, it is processed on your own device using JavaScript — it is never uploaded to our servers or seen by anyone else. You could disconnect from the internet mid-conversion and the tool would keep working.

🔒 We do use Google Analytics to understand how many people visit the site and which tools are most useful — but this only tracks anonymous usage patterns (like page views), never the contents of the files you process. See our Privacy Policy for details.

What's next

We're actively adding new tools and improving the existing ones based on what people actually need. If there's a tool you wish existed, or something that could work better, we'd like to hear about it.
